My Story

I graduated from Westwood College with a Graphic Design degree and landed a job as a graphic designer for Altawood Aerosols Inc. After 2 years I moved on to designing digital layouts for outdoor signs. I did that for about 4 years and went into marketing. This wasn't my thing.

I finally decided to dig really deep and try my hand at web development. I finished a bootcamp at Lambda's fullstack program and then got hired on a team doing web development. It has been one of the most challenging things I've ever done and also the most rewarding! I'm very proud of where I am at now and I very lucky to be doing something that I love.
